Features

🎶

Music Streaming

Play songs from YouTube and Spotify directly in your group voice chat. Supports URLs, search queries, playlists, albums, and Telegram media files.

📻

Live Radio

Tune into 33+ radio stations including Power Türk, Kral FM, Metro FM, Virgin Radio, Number1, and many more with inline button selection.

📺

Live TV

Stream 16+ TV channels in voice chats with video support. TRT 1, TV 8, Bloomberg HT, Halk TV, and more. Custom stream URLs are also supported.

⬇️

Music Download

Download songs as MP3 files directly in Telegram. Also supports downloading content from Instagram and TikTok.

🎤

Speech Tools

Text-to-speech, speech-to-text transcription with optional translation, and automatic language translation between any pair.

🎵

Lyrics Search

Find song lyrics powered by the Genius API. Just type the song name and get the full lyrics delivered to your chat.

📋

Playlists & Charts

Save favorite tracks to your personal playlist. View Global Top 10, Group Top 10, and Personal Top 10 charts, and play them instantly.

🔍

Inline Search

Type @AlemMuzikBot in any chat to search YouTube instantly. Up to 15 results with thumbnails, duration, and a direct play button.

🛡️

Group Management

Admin permissions, global ban system, blacklist management, mandatory channel membership, and automatic voice chat monitoring.

Playback Controls

Full control over your music sessions with intuitive commands.

Interactive Games

Play with your friends directly in group chats.

♟️

Chess

Full chess engine with board visualization, move validation, undo, checkmate & stalemate detection. Multiple simultaneous games supported.

❌⭕

Tic-Tac-Toe

Not your ordinary 3×3 grid. Choose 6×6 (4 in a row) or 8×8 (5 in a row) for an extra challenge. Played with inline buttons.

🗣️

Taboo

The classic word-guessing party game. 1,000+ words in Turkish & English. Voice and text modes. Leaderboards and achievements.

16 Languages

Each group sets its own language. Every button, menu, and message is fully translated.
